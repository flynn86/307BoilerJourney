	Stewart Center (STEW) is a multi-purpose building that can accommodate a variety of different needs through its four main floors containing 23 meeting rooms and two auditoriums. These two auditoriums, Loeb Playhouse and Fowler Hall can accommodate 1,400+ people total and have been home to music events, theatrical performances, and even a multitude of lectures and tests. STEW is also home to the Boilermaker Welcome Station and the Purdue Team Store, student organization offices, and other student-service offices.
