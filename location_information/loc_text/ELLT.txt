The Elliot Hall of Music (ELLT) is one of the largest proscenium theaters in the world. This theater can seat over 6,000 people and has three different levels, including two balconies. This hall is home too many different types of events including musicals, operas, ballets, rock bands, country artists, pop groups, comedians, lecturers, and symphonies. Some notable visitors of Elliot in the past have included Bob Dylan, Pearl Jam, Lady Gaga, Mark Morris Dance Group, Saint Petersburg Philharmonic Orchestra, Robin Williams, and Neil deGrasse Tyson. This theatre is not only just used for entertainment, it is also the location where many students will take their first tests at Purdue.
